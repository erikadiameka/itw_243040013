Fanny, one of the heroes in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB), is portrayed as a swift and agile assassin known for her impressive mobility using cables to zip through the battlefield. Here is a detailed origin story of Fanny: In the land of the Moniyan Empire, Fanny is the youngest of three siblings in the Vance family. She always admired her elder brother, Tigreal, who became a hero and the strongest warrior in the Moniyan Empire. However, Fanny herself felt limited by her human weaknesses. She longed to venture beyond the empire’s walls, unlike her brother, who was content serving within them. Her curiosity and determination fueled her desire to explore the world outside and make a name for herself. Determined to pursue her dreams and become as strong as her brother, Fanny trained day and night to improve her skills, especially her speed and agility. Despite her best efforts, she realized that she would never be able to match the strength of the monsters she might face on her journeys. However, instead of giving up, Fanny crafted a new fighting technique that relied on her agility and ingenuity. Inspired by the movements of the hawks she observed, she developed a grappling hook mechanism that allowed her to "fly" across the battlefield.

Lancelot is a Burst Assassin hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, renowned for his swift, elegant moves and exceptional burst damage. With his fencing style, Lancelot uses his agility and high damage output to pierce through enemies, making him deadly in the right hands. His skills allow him to maneuver around the battlefield with ease, quickly dispatching targets before retreating to safety. Lancelot was born into a noble family and known as the finest swordsman in the Land of Dawn. A skilled fencer, he was widely admired for his abilities, but his arrogance grew alongside his fame. However, he was ultimately defeated in a battle, leading him to reflect on his pride. He was then found and nursed back to health by Princess Odette of the Swan Castle. Over time, he fell in love with her and decided to better himself, adopting his skills to protect others rather than just seek glory. 2.
